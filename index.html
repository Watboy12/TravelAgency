    Frequently Asked Questions

    How do I open an account with ExploreWorld?

    Click on 'Create Account' in the top menu. Fill in the required information: your full name, a unique username, your email address, and a secure password (at least 8 characters with uppercase, lowercase, and numbers). Optional: Provide your phone number for better communication. Agree to our terms and conditions, then click 'Create Account'. After successfully registering, you’ll be redirected to the login page. Upon your first login, you’ll see a prompt to verify your identity by contacting our support team. This verification is required before you can book any trips. If you need assistance at any stage, our customer support team is here to help. Welcome to ExploreWorld—we’re thrilled to have you join our community of travelers! .

    What payment methods are accepted?

    We accept bank transfers and Bitcoin (BTC) deposits. Bank transfers require a verified name matching your account, while BTC deposits are credited within 10-60 minutes after confirmation.

    How long does it take to process a deposit?

    Bank deposits are processed within 1-3 business days, depending on your bank. Crypto deposits (BTC) are credited to your account within 10-60 minutes once the transaction is confirmed on the blockchain.

    Can I cancel or change my trip?

    Yes, cancellations or changes are possible up to 30 days before departure, subject to our terms. Contact our support team via the form below to discuss your options."

    What’s included in the deluxe packages?

    Deluxe packages vary by destination but typically include flights, premium accommodations, guided tours, and exclusive experiences. Check the ‘Learn More’ section for each destination for specifics.
